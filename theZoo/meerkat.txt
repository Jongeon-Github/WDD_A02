Meerkats are social and highly cooperative animals native to the deserts and grasslands of Africa.
Did you know?
- They are small mammals known for their upright posture and keen observation skills.
- Meerkat groups, called mobs or clans, work together to forage for food and protect their burrows from predators.
- They are known for their vigilant sentinels, which keep a lookout for danger while the others feed.
