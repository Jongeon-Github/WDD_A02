Tigers are among the most iconic and powerful big cats on Earth.
Did you know?
- They are apex predators known for their striking orange fur with black stripes.
- Tigers are carnivorous and hunt large mammals like deer and boars. Unfortunately, many tiger species are endangered due to habitat loss and poaching.
- Tigers symbolize strength and beauty and are often featured in various cultures' mythology and art.
- Conservation efforts are crucial to protect these magnificent creatures.
